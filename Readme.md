# 📊 E-commerce Sales Analysis using SQL

## 📌 Objective
Analyze sales data to extract meaningful business insights related to revenue, customer behavior, and product performance.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- MySQL

## 📂 Dataset
- Superstore Sales Dataset

## 📊 Analysis Performed
- Total Revenue Analysis
- Regional Sales Performance
- Customer Analysis
- Product & Category Analysis
- Monthly Sales Trends

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Total revenue of the dataset is 28,609.94
- The West region generates the highest revenue, while the South region contributes the least
- The Furniture category dominates overall sales
- Phones and Tables are the top-performing sub-categories
- A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue
- The Consumer segment accounts for the majority of sales
- Sales are concentrated among specific regions, products, and customers

## 🚀 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to analyze real-world datasets and extract actionable business insights.
